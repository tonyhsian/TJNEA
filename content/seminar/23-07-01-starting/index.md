<h4>Date:</h4> August 1, 2023
<h4>Location:</h4> Sendai Ryokusaikan Visitor Center, Sendai, Japan
Status: Satellite Event of the 46th Annual Meeting of the Japan Neuroscience Society
<p></p>
<p>
The 2023 Taiwan–Japan Neuroscience Exchange Symposium was held as an official satellite event of the 46th Annual Meeting of the Japan Neuroscience Society and was jointly organized with the Taiwan Neuroscience Society (TNS). 
</p>
<p>
Designed as an evening networking-focused symposium, the event combined brief introductory talks with extended social interaction. Speakers presented overviews of neuroscience research environments in Taiwan, Japan, and the international community, representing academic societies, universities, and research institutions.
</p>
<p>
The symposium concluded with an open networking session featuring food and drinks, encouraging informal discussion and cross-cultural exchange among participants from diverse backgrounds and career stages. This event established the foundation for TJNEA’s continued involvement in Taiwan–Japan neuroscience exchange activities.
</p>
